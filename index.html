FAQ

Got Questions?

How fast will my website be ready?

Starter: 72 hours. Standard: 48 hours. Premium: 24 hours. Need it faster? Rush delivery available.

What industries do you work with?

Any business that needs a website — restaurants, salons, gyms, real estate, medical practices, retail, e-commerce, consulting, and more. If you need a stunning online presence, we build it.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Your business name, a few photos, your services or products list, and any specific design preferences. Don't have everything ready? We can work with what you have.

What if I don't like the design?

We revise until you're satisfied. If after 2 revisions you still don't love it — full refund, no questions asked. That's our guarantee.

Can I update the site myself later?

Yes. We can set you up with easy editing access, or handle updates for you anytime. Premium plan includes 1 month of free updates.

What's the payment structure?

35% deposit to start the project. Remaining 65% due upon delivery — only when you're happy with the final result. We accept Wise, PayPal, and bank transfer.
