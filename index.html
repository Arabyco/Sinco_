FAQ

Got Questions?

How fast will my website be ready?

Starter: 72 hours. Standard: 48 hours. Premium: 24 hours. Rush delivery available on request.

What industries do you work with?

Any business that needs a website — restaurants, salons, gyms, real estate, medical, retail, e-commerce, consulting, and more.

What do I need to provide?

Your business name, photos, services list, and any design preferences. Don't have everything? We'll work with what you have.

What if I don't like the design?

We revise until you're satisfied. If after 2 revisions you still don't love it — full refund. That's our guarantee.

Can I update the site myself later?

Yes. We can set you up with easy editing access, or handle updates for you. Premium includes 1 month of free updates.

What's the payment structure?

35% deposit to start. Remaining 65% upon delivery — only when you're happy. We accept Wise, PayPal, and bank transfer.
